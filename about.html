We Make Difference in Your Homes

We have 10+ years of experiences for give you better quality results.

Everest Colour Ltd. is one of the Paint manufacturing company established on 2068 B.S. (2011 A.D.) in Nepal; involving of young and dynamic professionals from the same trade with different paints company. From 2068 B.S. ((2011 A.D.) to this day, in a brief span of time, particularly insignificant in the life of an industry and yet within this short period, it has emerged as a fore runner in Nepal's paint industry. Everest Colour Ltd. with various research and development (R & D) with modern technology provides its better quality products in the Nepalese paint market. The unit manufactures various Exterior and Interior Emulsion paints and primers, Enamel paints, Wood and metal primers, Acrylic Distempers, Aluminum paints, gold paints, Cement paint, Floor paint and Industrial Paints and so on.

Timeline of Everest Colour

2012 AD

The company was established by a group of youth professionals with the aim of providing high quality products with customer satisfaction and making the nation self-depend in the paint sector. The company registered as Everest Colour Pvt. Ltd. at Gatthaghar, Bhaktapur, Nepal with the high moral of becoming as the Mount Everest-Top of the Top.

2014 AD

In 2014, the factory was shifted to Sivaghat, Bharatpur, Chitwan with expanded area of 7,000 square feet for enhancing production and comfortable distribution of the products.

2018 AD

In the early morning of 30 May 2018, approx. 6:30 AM, a huge fire broke out into the factory due to electric short-circuit that completely destroyed the factory premises along with all the machines, raw-materials, packaging materials, finish products and other assets of the company.

2018 AD

Due to the fire, nothing was left except ashes. But within the 3 months of period,the production started again in just 600 square feet with the support of all the stakeholders of the company and the unforgettable leadership of the CEO and his motivated Team from Dharapani in Bharatpur, Chitwan from August 2018.

2019 AD

Hopes began to grow on the legacy of the company and its reputation for quality products. Now for mass production required land and its own building. The Company purchased 36450 sq.ft. land and started Factory construction in an area of 13500 sq.ft. Keeping aside the past, the product range has been expanded with innovative research and development. New products Bell Interior Emulsion and Eversine Exterior Emulsion have been launched.

2021 AD

Despite of COVID-19 and economical crisis in the country, new factory construction completed and entire factory shifted to new placeon the auspicious occasion of Bishwakarma Puja on 1st Aswin 2078. Production started with modern technology machines and well equipped modern laboratory. With research and development, new premium products like Everguard Premium Exterior Emulsion (with 7 years warranty) and Everlac Hi-Gloss Enamel has been launched in market. Meanwhile targeting the customer with low income, economic products like Evercoat Exterior Emulsion and Fuchche Bell Interior Emulsion has been developed and launched into the market.
